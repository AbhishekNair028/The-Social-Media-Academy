We cover Adobe Premiere Pro, After Effects, and DaVinci Resolve from absolute beginner to advanced professional levels. We also integrate cutting-edge AI tools for workflow optimization.
Do you teach video editing from basics? +
Yes. Module 1 is designed specifically for individuals who have never opened an editing timeline. We scale up rapidly, ensuring foundational strength before introducing advanced motion graphics and VFX.

For students in the Ultimate tier, we actively review portfolios and connect top performers with our network of agencies, YouTubers, and brands looking for elite editing talent.
